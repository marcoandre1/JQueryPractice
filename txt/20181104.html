I will try the converter now.
Ok, so a few things. Normally Word uses a margin between lines. When you copy paste the text that nice margin doesn’t stay. So the text is not going to appear as you see it in word. Second, you have to be careful of the options you chose. You should use the option “Use paragraph and line break tags”. Finally, it doesn’t support link references for websites for example. So, you must add those after you converted your file. It stills better than the attempt to save Word in html in my opinion.
